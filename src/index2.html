Tell me more

Born in Marsala (Italy), I studied Law in Pisa, and moved to Ireland in 2011, where I worked as a Sales Professional and Account Manager for about 6 years. I then moved to Madrid, about a year ago, as I was offered a position as a PS Account Manger at HomeAway.
  I have always been passionate about languages and computer science, which in a way, I believe can also be seen as a language itself and, most importantly, a great way to improve communication. However, due to having followed a different career path, I was never really able to give myself a real chance to focus on my passions. This is why, when I heard about "CEP - Engineering Application", I immediately got excited, and asked my manager if I could participate to it. She agreed,and I therefore jumped into the fascinating world of coding! I have to admit that it was harder than I thought it would be, but I also enjoyed it much more than I expected!
This project is a great opportunity for all of us who are sincerely curious about web-develpoment but are, at the same time, already employed and cannot afford to give it a chance on a full time basis. I really hope the auditing team will accept my application, and will give me a chance to explore this fantastic world even further!

P.S. I LOVE CODING! - If you read this, I might be a real developer! :)
